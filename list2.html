7 Best Pixar Movies

Here are my top 7 favorite (and therefore the best) Pixar Movies!

7. Coco (2017)

Directors: Lee Unkrich and Adrian Molina

An amazing buddy adventure. The ending is amazing. This movie would not be possible without the unique music and culture involved

6. Toy Story 3 (2010)

Director: Lee Unkrich

A solid ending to what should have been a trilogy. Woody and friends face their toughest challenge yet: moving on. Oh and also escpaing a maniacal purple bear

5. Finding Nemo (2003)

Directors: Andrew Stanton and Lee Unkrich

One of my favorites when I was a kid. A story about a father and son learning to love and appreciate each other in spite of everything.

4. Soul (2020)

Directors: Pete Docter and Kemp Powers

A unique look at what makes a life worth living. It will make you examine how you make the most of your life.

3. WALL*E (2008)

Director: Andrew Stanton

Robots in love! Also a look at what will happen to the planet if we don't take care of it and ourselves.

2. Incredibles (2004)

Director: Brad Bird

A story about a family learning to come and work together. Also one of the best superhero movies of all time.

1. Ratatouille (2007)

Director: Brad Bird

The Magum Opus. The Pixar movie. Looks at dreams and achieving them. And appreciating art.
