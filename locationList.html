The 7 best NBA arenas in America

The NBA has 29 arenas in the United States. Here are the best ones.

Intuit Dome

The newest arena in the NBA, the Intuit Dome, has gotten rave reviews. With plenty of bathrooms and the famous “The Wall.”

Address: 3903 W. Century Blvd, Inglewood, CA 90303

(888)311-0231

Link to the Intuit Dome

United Center

The House that Jordan built is the arena with the most seats. With its games being some of the most attended.

Address: 1901 W. Madison St., Chicago, IL 60612

(312)455-4500

Link to the United Center

Madison Square Garden

Madison Square Garden has always gotten rave reviews from the players.

Address: 4 Penn Plaza, New York, NY 10121

(212)465-6000

Link to Madison Square Garden

Paycom Center

Oklahoma City has some of the most passionate basketball fans, with their games always having some great pops

Address: 100 W. Reno Ave, Oklahoma City, OK, 73102

(405)602-8700

Link to Paycom Center

Crypto.com Arena

The Home of the Lakers, every visiting team always plays their best when play on this court

Address: 1111 S. Figueroa St., Los Angeles, CA, 90015

(213)742-7100

Link to Crypto.com Arena

Barclays Center

The home of the Brooklyn Nets, this arena also hosts the annual NBA Draft.

Address: 620 Atlantic Ave., Brooklyn, NY, 11217

(917)618-6100

Link to Barclays Center

TD Garden

The Garden always has its fans show up for them.

Address: 100 Legends Way, Boston, MA, 02114

(617)624-1000

Link to TD Garden
